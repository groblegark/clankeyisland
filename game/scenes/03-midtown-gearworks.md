# Scene 03 — Midtown Gearworks (Main Street)

**Act:** 2 · **Background:** `assets/backgrounds/midtown.png` (320x200, scrolling 640 wide)

The neon heart of Clanker City. A double-wide scrolling street scene — the
game's hub for Act 2. Giant rotating gear-clock tower in the center
(it has visibly slowed; citizens pretend not to notice).

## Locations on the street

1. **Lefty's Spare Parts** — shop run by Lefty, a bot with two left arms.
   Sells the talent-show props.
2. **The Oil Bar** — upscale cousin of the Scrap & Barrel. Cocktails like the
   "Crankshaft Sour." Gossip source.
3. **City Hall** — Mayor Piston's office. Locked behind a receptionist-bot
   with infinite bureaucratic forms (Form 27B/6 gag).
4. **Grand Cog Theater** — marquee: "TALENT NIGHT — WINNER MEETS MADAME
   VOLTINA." Act 2's spine.
5. **Gear-clock tower** — examine to track story progress; it slows each act.

## Act 2 spine

Enter talent show → need an act → three options (pick any, all comedic):
- **Juggling** (buy magnets from Lefty, "juggle" them — they orbit your head)
- **Comedy** (collect three jokes from Oil Bar patrons; they're all terrible;
  the robot audience finds terrible puns *hilarious*)
- **Escape artist** (use Rustler handcuffs from Act 1 + your own detachable hand)

Win → backstage pass → Madame Voltina's dressing room → she'll trade
**Key #2** for proof the Mayor is lying (the torn poster from Scene 01 +
City Hall's real maintenance log, stolen via the Form 27B/6 puzzle).
